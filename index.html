C2C4 Scholar Program and Dual Enrollment - Fall 2012

During my senior year of high school I decided to dual enroll at University of West Georgia and start my college classes early. The C2C4 Science Scholar Program at the University of West Georgia allows select high school students to utilize the university's libraries, lab-space, and instrumentation in order to develop competitive, state of the art Science Fair Projects, and the opportunity to be mentored by professors.
